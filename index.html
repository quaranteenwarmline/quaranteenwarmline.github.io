QuaranTeen Warmline

A youth dedicated warmline for teenagers between the ages 15-18. Avaliable everyday through call (4pm to 10pm IST) and DM (10am-10pm IST)

About us Contact us

When do you call a warmline?

A warmline is a number you call when you need to speak with someone you will listen to your concerns without judgement or criticism. During a time where we are faced with unprecedented levels of uncertainty, it’s common to feel worry, anxiety and stress. The warm-line offers an empathetic ear to people who simply need to talk to someone who will understand what they are going through. Staffed by teenagers - the warm-line aims to offer peer to peer telephone support during this global pandemic.Teenagers are often afraid they’ll be judged for what they’re going through or how they’re feeling. That's why it's easier to talk to friends and other people your age for support because we understand what you are going through

What is peer to peer support?

Peer supporters serve as someone you can talk to who understands what you are going through, a peer supporter is someone who can relate to what you are going thriugh and can help you through shared experiences. 

Disclaimers 

  1. The Warmline is meant for teenagers between the ages of 15-18, please use the service only if you fall under this catogary 
  2. Peer supporters are not licenced professionals, we cannot give advice n how to sure/deal with serious mental illnesses 
  3. The warmline is not a helpline or a hotline, Callers experiencing emergencies or very serious issues are not advised to use this warmline.
