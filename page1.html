QuaranTeen Warmline

A youth dedicated line for teenagers between the ages 15-18

The QuaranTeen Warmline uses peer to peer suport to lend an empathetic ear to teenagers dealing with COVID-19 stress, academic pressure, social issues or are simply just feeling lonely and need someone to talk to. We are currently the only Warmline in India and the only COVID-19 related warmline in the world
